About Aagaaz

Aagaaz is a platform born out of my passion for math and puzzles, designed to challenge and sharpen minds. Whether you're solving intricate Sudoku puzzles or tackling brain-teasing math quizzes in Mathemania, Aagaaz offers fun and learning for all skill levels.

Key Features:

  • Customizable Sudoku puzzles with Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulties
  • Interactive math and reasoning quizzes in Mathemania
  • Progress tracking with history for both games
  • Sleek, responsive dark mode interface
  • Built with Flask and Python for a seamless experience

Game Details

Sudoku

Test your logic with our Sudoku solver and checker. Choose from three difficulty levels, fill in the 9x9 grid, and use the solve or check features to perfect your puzzle.

How to Play:

  • Select a difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard) to generate a puzzle
  • Enter numbers (1-9) in the grid
  • Use "Solve" to auto-complete or "Check" to validate your solution
  • Track your progress with the history feature

Mathemania

Challenge your brain with math and reasoning quizzes. From arithmetic to logic puzzles, Mathemania offers 10-question sets across three difficulty levels.

How to Play:

  • Choose a difficulty to start a 10-question quiz
  • Select the correct answer from four options
  • Navigate with Previous/Next buttons
  • View your score and history after completing the quiz
